Privacy Policy

Your privacy. Our responsibility.

We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your data.

Effective Date: 01 May 2025
1. Information We Collect

We collect personal info, verification details, financial information, technical data, and location data to provide our services.

2. How We Use Info

We use your data to manage accounts, process orders and payments, ensure deliveries, detect fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Info

We share necessary data with delivery partners, payment gateways, and vendors to facilitate your orders. We do not sell your personal data.

4. Security & Retention

We use industry best practices to protect your data and retain it only as long as your account is active or as required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

Our Privacy Policy in Detail

To provide and improve our services, we collect various types of information, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, shipping address, and profile credentials.
  • Verification Details: Government identifiers or business registration details if required for verification.
  • Financial Information: Payment method preferences and billing addresses. We do not store full payment card numbers.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device type, operating system, browser data, and usage statistics.
  • Location Data: Estimated or precise geographic location based on IP address or device settings.

We process your data for the following purposes:

  • To set up and manage user accounts on SabkaFayda.
  • To process, package, and deliver your orders successfully.
  • To facilitate secure transactions and prevent payment fraud.
  • To analyze user engagement to optimize application performance.
  • To fulfill regulatory tax and billing compliance requirements mandated by law.

We value your privacy and share your data only in the following scenarios:

  • Service Providers: We share details with shipping companies and logistics partners to deliver your packages, and payment processors to complete transactions.
  • Compliance & Legal: If requested by legal authorities or to resolve billing disputes, we cooperate in compliance with regional laws.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell, lease, or rent your personal user profiles to advertising networks.

To protect your information, we employ the following guidelines:

  • Data Protection: We use encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) for data transfer, and secure authentication to prevent unauthorized account access.
  • Retention: We retain your information as long as your account remains active or to fulfill financial compliance (such as GST audit trail guidelines).

You have full control over your personal data. You are entitled to:

  • Access a copy of the personal information stored in our databases.
  • Correct inaccurate, outdated, or incomplete details in your user profile.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data, subject to legal and regulatory retention requirements.
  • For inquiries or to exercise these rights, please email us directly at support@teammunity.in.
