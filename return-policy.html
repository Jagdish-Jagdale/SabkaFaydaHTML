Return & Exchange Policy

At SabkaFayda, customer satisfaction is important to us. To ensure a fair and transparent process, please carefully read our Return & Exchange Policy.

Section 1

Return & Exchange Window

Customers may request a return or exchange within 24 hours of product delivery. Requests submitted after 24 hours from the delivery time will not be accepted.

Mandatory Unboxing Video

A complete and uninterrupted unboxing video is compulsory for all return and exchange requests. Video must show: - Sealed package - Shipping label - Product being unboxed - Condition received

Section 2

Eligible Reasons

Requests may be considered for: - Damaged product - Wrong product - Missing item - Defective product

Non-Eligible Cases

Not accepted for: - Change of mind - Personal preference - Incorrect selection - Requests without video - Requests after 24h - Products damaged after delivery

Section 3

Approval Process

All requests subject to verification by our support team. Provide: - Unboxing video - Product images - Order details

Refund Process

Approved refunds processed via original payment or appropriate method. Timelines vary by provider.

Exchange Process

Approved exchanges subject to product availability. If unavailable, SabkaFayda may offer refund or alternative.

Rights Reserved

We reserve right to approve, reject, investigate requests. All decisions are final and binding.
