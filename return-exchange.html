Return & Exchange Policy

Hassle-free returns and exchanges for your peace of mind

Return Policy

Easy returns within 7 days of delivery

Return Eligibility
  • Product must be unused and in original packaging
  • Return request within 7 days of delivery
  • Original price tag and invoice must be intact
  • Product should not be damaged or defective
Non-Returnable Items
  • Personal care and hygiene products
  • Undergarments and intimate apparel
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Products marked as "Non-Returnable"
Return Process
  1. Go to "My Orders" and select the order
  2. Click on "Return" and select reason
  3. Schedule pickup or self-drop
  4. Refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

Exchange Policy

Exchange for different size or color within 10 days

Exchange Eligibility
  • Exchange within 10 days of delivery
  • Same product in different size/color
  • Product must be in original condition
  • Price difference will be adjusted
Free Exchange
  • Free pickup for first exchange
  • No extra charges for size/color change
  • Instant exchange at select locations
  • Doorstep exchange service available
Exchange Process
  1. Select the product from "My Orders"
  2. Click on "Exchange" and choose new variant
  3. Confirm exchange and schedule pickup
  4. New product will be delivered in 3-5 business days

Refund Policy

Quick refunds to your original payment method

Processing Time

5-7 business days after product pickup

Refund Method

Original payment method or store credit

Secure Refund

100% secure and guaranteed refund
