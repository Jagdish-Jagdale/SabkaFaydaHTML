FAQ - Customer Data Privacy

Q: How does Sabka Fayda ensure my data privacy?

A: We employ advanced encryption and strictly controlled access to safeguard all your personal and financial information.

Q: What steps are in place to verify data access requests?

A: Before accessing sensitive data, our system performs multifactor authentication (including codes sent to your registered mobile number) to ensure the request is from you.

Q: Can my data be shared with other sellers or partners?

A: No, your primary contact details are masked from individual sellers. Limited data required for services (like delivery addresses to logistics partners) is shared under strict data processing agreements.

Q: How do I control my marketing preferences?

A: You can easily manage all your consent settings directly within your Sabka Fayda profile.

Q: Can I access or delete my saved data?

A: Yes, users can request access to or deletion of their personal data directly through the account settings.

Q: What are your data retention practices?

A: We retain your account data only for as long as necessary to provide seamless services and fulfill security requirements.

At SabkaFayda, customer satisfaction remains our highest priority and we continuously strive to deliver the best shopping experience possible.
