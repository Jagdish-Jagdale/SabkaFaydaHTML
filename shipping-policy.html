Shipping Policy

At SabkaFayda, we strive to deliver your products in the fastest and most transparent way. Please read our detailed Shipping Policy below.

SECTION 1

PROCESSING TIMES

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a confirmation email with details.

ORDER MODIFICATION

Changes to shipping address can be requested within 12 hours of order placement.

SECTION 2

DELIVERY TIME ESTIMATES

Domestic: 3-7 business days. International: 7-21 business days. All estimates are indicative.

FAILED DELIVERY ATTEMPTS

Up to three delivery attempts. Uncollected packages will be returned and subject to restocking fees.

SECTION 3

TRACKING YOUR SHIPMENT

Once shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email and SMS. Track on our website or carrier site.

SHIPMENT STATUS UPDATES

Receive real-time status updates from order confirmation to final delivery.

SECTION 4

SHIPPING RATES

Rates are calculated based on weight, dimensions, destination, and selected service. Displayed at checkout.

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING & CUSTOMS

Customers are responsible for any destination country customs duties and taxes.
